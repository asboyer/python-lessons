Welcome to the mystical land of The Wild Vales, a place that was once filled with joy, magic, and creativity. 

Unfortunately, those times passed on centuries ago when the evil wizard, Orlatrix, invaded the most powerful kingdom at the time, Meamivar, and became the emperor of the Wild Vales. The dark times are now upon this land, but there is hope. 

A fortnight ago, the outcast sorceress, Ofora, prophesied that a new hero would restore glory back to the Wild Vales....
